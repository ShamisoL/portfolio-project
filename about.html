About me

Shamiso Lewis

Hello and welcome to Shamiso Stitches! I'm Shamiso, a Systems specialist by day and a hoop embroidery artist by night. I picked up embroidery in lockdown to see what all the fuss was about and then got hooked! I started off with DIY hoop embroidery kits and then started making my own designs. Nothing gives me more joy than making a bespoke pieces for people, taking their ideas and seeing it come to life on the fabric.
